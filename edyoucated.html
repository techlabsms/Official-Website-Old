Our Open Curriculum

introducing edyoucated and a new way to learn

Previously, access to our tech curriculum was only possible through participation in the official TechLabs Journey. Now, however, our TechLabs curriculum team has invested plenty of time to identify the best supporting resources to enable you to acquire tech skills as efficiently as possible.


In the Track Guides, you find a comprehensive list of valuable resources for our three learning tracks Data Science with Python, Artificial Intelligence and Web Development (e.g. cheat sheets, freely accessible books, helpful articles, sample code).

Furthermore you can now get access to our tracks via edyoucated. edyoucated is a revolutionary platform that significantly improves the user experience along the learning process of (digital) skills. In the future you can use edyoucated to plan and track your learning processes and share your learning concepts with others.
