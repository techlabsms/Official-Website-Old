Bring TechLabs to your City

You always wanted to become active yourself and build something from scratch?


TechLabs' mission is to create a large community of tech-savvy people who share a common goal: To solve the problems of our time digitally by enabling efficient learning of tech topics for everyone.

You can identify with this mission? You can imagine starting TechLabs in your own city?

What's in it for you?

TechLabs is a well-established association with various subsidiaries across Europe. This offers you unique advantages throughout your journey as a TechLabs founder:

  • You become part of an organization that is rethinking digital learning and is therefore already on its way to becoming a pioneer in tech education
  • You get the opportunity to take on responsibility and develop yourself personally as well as discover new topics and skills for yourself
  • We provide you with a ready-to-go, validated and awarded (winner of the Google Impact Challenge 2018) concept for a highly relevant area with extensive growth potential
  • You can found, design and lead your very own TechLabs subsidiary and advance our vision with your personal touch
  • Your get access to the rich knowledge base and best practices of the TechLabs family, with a broad network of young, motivated and driven people who want to make an impact
    • compiled and customizable curricula, track guides, explanatory videos, best practices on a variety of topics including marketing, partner acquisition and more
    • sophisticated and validated semester plan which covers all aspects of the TechLabs journey
    • Carefully selected tools to ensure the most efficient flow of operations (HubSpot, Slack, Notion, G-Suite and even our very own online learning platform)
  • Benefit from our network of excellent mentors and renowned partner companies such as zeb, Accenture Interactive, Westfalen AG or McKinsey.

So, who are we looking for?

  • ...are going to stay at your location for at least one more year to lead the association
  • ...are passionate about technology, inspire others and completely identify with the TechLabs mission
  • ...are able to represent the association professionally to the outside in order to build a local TechLabs community
  • ...have prior experience as board member of a (student) association or have worked in an early-stage startup (not necessarily, but a plus)

You can totally identify with our mission but there is no TechLabs subsidiary in your city (yet)? Let us know and we will get in touch with you right away!
