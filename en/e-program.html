The TechLabs Program

TechLabs offers three tracks for your digital education!


The benefits at a glance

  • Based on your previous knowledge, we will provide you with a personalized curriculum of high quality and field-proven online courses. Whether you are a complete beginner programmer or already advanced, everyone can join in and continue her education!
  • At least every two weeks during the semester a meeting of the TechLabs-Community takes place in M√ºnster. Get to know other tech-interested participants from different fields!
  • Visits Interesting lectures by various speakers and workshops of our well-known business partners.
  • Apply your newly acquired Tech Skills directly to an exciting project that you'll continue to advance with other TechLabs participants. Best of all, contribute your own project ideas and do the project you enjoy the most!
  • Specialists and Mentors with practical experience support you continuously during the semester with technical or project-related questions.

TechLabs semester schedule


Orientation Week
  • Explore all important details about our three different learning tracks Web Development, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Onboarding Event. Also get information about our special TechLabs-learning concept.
  • Get to know all the other participants in the TechLabs community.
  • Get helpful tips and valuable resources in your Track Guide that will guide you through your TechLabs semester learning experience.
  • Answer a first questionnaire to choose your learning track and your previous knowledge, so that we can create an optimal individual experience for you.
TechLabs Academy
  • Our meetings usually take place Wednesdays, every 2 weeks. More about this on our info evening, which you can view via Facebook. :-)
  • Get a personalized curriculum from online courses in the track of your choice. Whether you are a complete novice programmer or already advanced, through our individualized learning paths, anyone can further develop their tech skills!
  • Use our own online learning platform to view your learning path, check off your learning progress and get memories for learning. The learning platform is also constantly updated with new features.
  • visit our TechLabs community meetings, which usually take place every two weeks (e.g. workshops, lectures, socials, mini hackathons).
  • Apply your newly learnt Tech Skills in the semester directly to a practice project of your choice, which you will complete in an interdisciplinary group.

After your semester with us: TechLabs Alumni Community
  • Profit long-term from our network to companies and partners.
